

 Q1.Explain the meaning of the deadlock in multithreaded application, when it can occur in multithreading applications.Write one example of 
     deadlock.
     
 Answer) Deadlock is basically a situation in where two or more threads are blocked forever waiting for each other. Deadlock occurs when multiple 
         threads need same locks but obtain them in a different order. In multithreadeing we get the deadlock condition because of synchronized 
         keyword.
